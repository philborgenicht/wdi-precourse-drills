All About Me

I am originally from Long Valley, New Jersey.
Growing up I liked to do normal stuff: play sports,
listen to music, hang out with my friends,
play videogames, and go to the movies.
My favorite sports are baseball and basketball
and my favorite teams are the New York Yankees
and the Los Angeles Lakers.

My three best friends growing up, Tom, Dave, and Kevin
are still my best friends to this day. Even though we
all live in different states, we do our best to meet up
a few times a year. This past march we were able to all get
together in Pennsylvania for Dave's wedding, which was quite fun.



My Family

One of the coolest things that recently happened
to me was becoming an uncle. The main reason I moved
to Colorado with my mom was to be able to be with my sister
as she and her husband started to have kids and raise a family.
Being an uncle is pretty much all the fun of being a parent,
but with only like 3-4 percent of the responsibility.

Luckily, I live within walking distance of my sister's house
so I try to go over several times per week and hang out.
